// the solution

Engineered for Silence

MuteBand uses advanced ultrasonic technology to create an invisible shield around your conversations.

Ultrasonic Shield

Emits precision-tuned ultrasonic frequencies that overload microphone sensors, making your voice unrecordable by any nearby device.

One-Tap Control

Activate or deactivate your privacy shield instantly with a single tap. No apps, no pairing, no complexity — just immediate protection.

Stealth Design

Looks like a premium bracelet. No one will know you're wearing a privacy device — complete discretion in any setting.

Room-Wide Protection

Covers the space around you — your desk, your dinner table, your meeting room. Every microphone in range is neutralized the moment you activate.

// simple by design

How It Works

Privacy protection should be effortless. MuteBand works in three simple steps.

Wear It

Slide MuteBand onto your wrist. Lightweight, comfortable, and designed to look like a premium bracelet — not a gadget.

Tap to Activate

A single tap activates ultrasonic emission. The subtle LED confirms you are shielded. One more tap to deactivate.

You're Protected

Every microphone within range is neutralized. Speak freely, knowing your words stay between the people in the room.
