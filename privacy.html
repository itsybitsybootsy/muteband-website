Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

01 Who we are

MuteBand is a student business project by Lucas Lammer, Teoman Civici, Yoto Krastev, and William Ye, developed at Theresianum Wien, Favoritenstraße 15, 1040 Wien, Austria.

Contact: info@muteband.eu

02 What data we collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The date and time of your submission

We do not collect any other personal data. We do not use cookies for tracking purposes.

03 Why we collect it (Legal basis)

We collect your email address based on your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) when you voluntarily submit it through our waitlist form. The purpose is to notify you about MuteBand product updates and availability.

04 How we store it

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), operated by Google LLC. Google Firebase servers may be located in the EU or US. Google is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. For more information, see Google's privacy policy.

05 Who has access

Only the MuteBand project team members (listed above) have access to the collected email addresses. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any other third parties.

06 How long we keep it

We retain your email address for a maximum of 2 years from the date of collection, or until the school project concludes, whichever comes first. After this period, all collected data will be permanently deleted.

07 Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Rectify inaccurate data (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Request deletion of your data (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Restrict the processing of your data (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Request portability of your data (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Withdraw your consent at any time (Art. 7(3) GDPR)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (Datenschutzbehörde, www.dsb.gv.at)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@muteband.eu.

08 Third-party services

  • Google Fonts: All fonts are self-hosted on our server. No data is transmitted to Google for font loading.
  • Google Firebase: The Firebase SDK is self-hosted on our server. Firebase Firestore is used for storing waitlist submissions. When you submit the waitlist form, your email is sent to Google Firebase servers. See section 4 for details.

09 Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

10 Note

This is a school project developed at Theresianum Wien. The website and its data collection serve educational and demonstration purposes and may not lead to a commercial product.
